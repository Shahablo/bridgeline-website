Process

A focused search process for Epic teams that are already moving.

Every engagement starts with the workstream, not the resume. That keeps the shortlist small, relevant, and easier to approve.

Step 01 / 24-48 hrs

Intake

Map module, version, timeline, team structure, location model, and what breaks if the role stays open.

Step 02 / 3-5 days

Match

Search the bench for consultants with relevant production build, training, go-live, or RCM experience.

Step 03 / Included

Validate

Run Epic-specific screens and submit concise fit notes with rates, availability, and constraints.

Step 04 / Week 1

Deploy

Coordinate onboarding, credentials, expectations, and early check-ins so the consultant starts cleanly.

FAQ

Questions buyers ask before the first call.

If the answer depends on module, urgency, or work model, we will tell you that directly during intake.

How fast can you send candidates?

For well-scoped urgent needs, the first qualified shortlist is typically 3-5 days after intake.

Do you cover all Epic modules?

We support the major Epic ecosystem across clinical, revenue cycle, access, reporting, and training roles.

Can you support go-lives?

Yes. We place analysts, trainers, at-the-elbow support, project managers, and activation support roles.

Are rates transparent?

Yes. We discuss rates, work model, availability, and conversion terms before submitting candidates.

Do you place W-2 or 1099 consultants?

Both. We structure engagements around client requirements and consultant preference where possible.
